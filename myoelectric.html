Myoelectric Technology

Advanced prosthetic control through muscle signals

How It Works

Myoelectric technology uses electrical signals from your muscles to control prosthetic movements. When you think about moving your hand or arm, your muscles generate tiny electrical signals. Our advanced sensors detect these signals and translate them into precise prosthetic movements.

  • Real-time muscle signal processing
  • Intuitive control system
  • Advanced pattern recognition
